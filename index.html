The meaning of the term health and fitness has evolved over a period of time. In 1948, WHO coined the new definition of health and fitness which basically relates to the state of well-being rather than a state of the body.

The majority of people are involved in jobs where they have to sit all through the day and are facing the problems of obesity and tiredness. The lifestyle choices like smoking cigarettes, drinking alcohol, overeating is the main driving factors for poor health and fitness.
